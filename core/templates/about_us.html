Hada Logistics

Hadad Logistics is a fast-growing logistic business, with it’s operating base in Abuja, Nigeria. Hadad Logistics is a subsidiary of Hadad Business Group, which was founded in 2016. At Hadad Logistics, we see it as part of our duty to help co-ordinate all your logistic needs, by providing our clients with swift, efficient, and reliable services, with our cutting-edge technology. At Hadad Logistics, we make customers satisfaction our priority. We handle all of your logistic needs and make appropriate adjustments as schedules shift. Transportation, chauffeured vehicles for rent, taxi cab services, chauffeur for hire, errand services, and trucking services are only a few services we provide.

Our Services

  1. City Lift;
    This I the fastest way to get around town. Our pick-ups and drop-offs are simple, safe, fashionable, and convenient. If you think you would be making several stops, you can book for an hour or more.

  2. Airport eXpress/ Airport trip;
    This service will bring you to and from the airport quickly and safe. Our drivers are dependable, productive, and punctual. They use a flight tracking device to ensure that they get you to the airport on time, and are ready to meet you as soon as you arrive, even at odd hours of the day.

    This corporate service is appropriate for professionals in corporate organizations who have a long to-do list that needs to be completed in a single day. This service also provides you a licensed chauffeur for 10hrs.

  4. Bon Voyage:
    This is what you need if you are looking to go on a road trip with seasoned drivers who know the best and safest routes. Our destination spans through various states in the country. To schedule this service, give us a call right now.

This service also provides you with an optional security personnel who is experienced and well trained, and optional meal plan.

    Are you looking for a way to get around for your wedding, birthday party, bachelor party, or some other events? With our Comfort, Executive or Luxury services we’ve got you covered. We’re just a phone call away.

  7. Errand Services:
    We provide fast, secure, effective and dependable errand services. With the aid of our dispatch bikes and up to date technologies, we take care of your pick-up and delivery needs in a timely manner.

  8. Trucking:
    We transport your Cargo and bulk services from point A-B with our trucking services. We are dependable, secure and effective. You can count on us to stay consistent in our timely delivery.
